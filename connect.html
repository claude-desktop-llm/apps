Step-by-step guide

Never used Terminal before? Start here.

These four steps walk you through installing Claude from scratch. The whole process takes about a minute, and you don’t need any prior experience with Terminal.

1

Find and open Terminal

Terminal comes pre-installed on every Mac. Here are two ways to open it:

Option A — Spotlight: Press the keyboard shortcut below, type Terminal, and hit Enter.

⌘ Cmd+Space

Option B — Finder: Open Finder → Applications → Utilities → Terminal.

2

Copy the installation command

Use the Copy button to copy the command, or select it manually with your cursor:

$Loading...
3

Paste the command and run it

Click anywhere inside the Terminal window to focus it, paste the command, then press Enter to start the installation.

⌘ Cmd+VthenEnter ⏎
$curl -fsSL https://cli.anthropic.com/install | sh

4

Claude launches automatically

Once installation finishes, Claude opens by itself. Sign in with your Anthropic account or register a free one to begin. The app is also added to your Applications folder and Dock for quick access.

Pro tip: After installing, you can open Claude anytime with Spotlight — press ⌘ Cmd + Space, type Claude, and hit Enter.
